Challenge 2

Speed Detector Code Challenge

Click the button below to get a prompt that enables you to input the speed of the car. The speed limit it 70 km/s. If the speed is equal to or below 70, an alert displaying 'Ok' will be displayed. However, if the speed is above the limit, your demerit points will be displayed. You will then receive an alert indicating 'License suspended' if your demerit points exceed 12.
